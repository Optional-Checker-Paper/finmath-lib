23    /**
24    * Displaced log-normal process with GARCH(1,1) volatility.
25    *
26    * This class estimate the process
27    * \[
28    * \mathrm{d} \log(X + a) = \frac{\sigma}{b + a} \mathrm{d}W(t)
29    * \]
30    * where \( a &gt; -min(X(t_{i}) \) and thus \( X+a &gt; 0 \) and \( b = 1 - -min(X(t_{i}) \) \) and
31    * \( \sigma \) is given by a GARCH(1,1) process.
32    *
33    * The choice of b ensures that b+a &ge; 1.
34    * For a=0 we have a log-normal process with volatility &sigma;/(b + a).
35    * For a=infinity we have a normal process with volatility &sigma;.

37    * @author Christian Fries
