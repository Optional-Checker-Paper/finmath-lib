16    /**
17    * Implementation of a forward given by a Nelson-Siegel-Svensson (NSS) parameterization.
18    * In the NSS parameterization the zero rate \( r(T) \) is given by
19    *
20    * \[ r(T) = \beta_0 + \beta_1 \frac{1-x_0}{T/\tau_0} + \beta_2 ( \frac{1-x_0}{T/\tau_0} - x_0) + \beta_3 ( \frac{1-x_1}{T/\tau_1} - x_1) \]
21    *
22    * where \( x_0 = \exp(-T/\tau_0) \) and \( x_1 = \exp(-T/\tau_1) \).
23    *
24    * The sub-family of curves with \( \beta_3 = 0 \) is called Nelson-Siegel parameterization.
25    *
26    * @see net.finmath.marketdata.model.curves.DiscountCurveNelsonSiegelSvensson
27    * @author Christian Fries
28    * @version 1.0
29    */
