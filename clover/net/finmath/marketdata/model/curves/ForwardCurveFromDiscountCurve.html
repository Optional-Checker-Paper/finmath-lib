17    /**
18    * A forward curve derived from a given discount curve.
19    *
20    * The forward with fixing in \( t \) is calculated as
21    * \(
22    * (\frac{1}{dcf(t,t+d)} ( \frac{df(t)}{df(t+d)}-1 )
23    * \)
24    * where \( dcf(t,t+d) \) is the daycount-fraction between t and t+d and \( t \mapsto df(t) \) is the given referenceDiscountCurveNameForForwardCurve.
25    * The payment offset \( t+d \) is either generated from the paymentOffsetCode or directly specified if paymentOffsetCode=NaN.
26    *
27    * The referenceDiscountCurveNameForForwardCurve is referenced by name and evaluated late (which allows use of
28    * this construct in a calibration process referencing changing discount curves.
29    *
30    * @author Christian Fries
31    * @version 1.0
32    */
