17    /**
18    * This class implements a Black Scholes Model, that is, it provides the drift and volatility specification
19    * and performs the calculation of the numeraire (consistent with the dynamics, i.e. the drift).
20    *
21    * The model is
22    * \[
23    * dS = r S dt + \sigma S dW, \quad S(0) = S_{0},
24    * \]
25    * \[
26    * dN = r N dt, \quad N(0) = N_{0},
27    * \]
28    *
29    * The class provides the model of S to an <code>{@link net.finmath.montecarlo.process.MonteCarloProcess}</code> via the specification of
30    * \( f = exp \), \( \mu = r - \frac{1}{2} \sigma^2 \), \( \lambda_{1,1} = \sigma \), i.e.,
31    * of the SDE
32    * \[
33    * dX = \mu dt + \lambda_{1,1} dW, \quad X(0) = \log(S_{0}),
34    * \]
35    * with \( S = f(X) \). See {@link net.finmath.montecarlo.process.MonteCarloProcess} for the notation.
